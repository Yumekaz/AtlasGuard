## Design Decisions

1. **Pure scoring in shared package** — `computeRiskScore` is framework-agnostic and unit-tested; API service only gathers inputs (zones via existing `findMatchingZones`, responders, nearby incidents).
2. **Scoring rules per Bible §17.3** — HIGH +25, CRITICAL +30, night +10, MEDICAL type +20, medical notes +10, responder >5 km +10, nearby incidents >3 within 2 km +10; capped at 100 with human-readable `reasons[]`.
3. **Severity derived from score** — bands 0–30 LOW, 31–55 MEDIUM, 56–80 HIGH, 81–100 CRITICAL via `scoreToSeverity`.
4. **Dashboard analytics** — `averageResponseTimeMinutes` computed from incident `createdAt` to first `ACKNOWLEDGED` event; `criticalCount` = active CRITICAL severity count.
5. **Demo scenario** — `simulate-demo` reuses seed helper, cancels/resolves open incidents, sets asthma medical notes, moves responder far from CRITICAL zone (>5 km) to maximize demo risk score, returns playbook with `DEMO_LOCATIONS.remoteNorth`.
6. **Seed refactor** — `admin/seed` and `simulate-demo` share `seedDemoAccounts` to avoid duplication.
